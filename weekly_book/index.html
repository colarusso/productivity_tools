If you have reoccurring appointments, you can add them to the days below and not only will they show up on your printout, they should be remembered the next time you visit this page. Of course, you can just write things in by hand if you prefer. To make your book, print this page double sided. These instructions shouldn't show up. Once you have your sheet, fold it such that the cover and back pages are on the outside, cut, and staple. If you want to edit a page beyond the schedule, fork this repo and edit the index.html file found in the weekly_book folder.
